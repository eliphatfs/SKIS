ttty (tele-terminal-typewriter)

Remotely visit your terminal. This may be useful for e.g. remote debugging from a CI worker, or when you want to share resources with your cooperator.

To begin with this service:

  1. Install websockets, pycryptodomex and tqdm for your python.
  2. If you are hosting a terminal from MacOS or non-x64 devices, install ttyd (brew install ttyd for MacOS).
  3. Run client/ttty.py on your target device. (add --help for usage)
  4. Connect into the device here.
  5. All data transferred over SKIS will be encrypted with a hash of your provided password. Do not use a weak password!
